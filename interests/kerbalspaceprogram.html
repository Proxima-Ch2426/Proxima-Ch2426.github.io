Huge thanks to my dad for purchasing this game for me. Around the time of purchase, I did not know much about rocketry or areospace in general, but I think this game definitely gave rise to that interest. I didn't really play much during the first few years, but I started playing it much more from 2020 onwards, partly due to Covid and partly due to the Starship program. As of writing, I am now a modder for this game and occasionally do play throughs with the realism mod suite.
