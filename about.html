Introduction

On the internet, you can call me Proxima or just Proxi for short. I am an artist and future areospace engineer currently living in Singapore, making 3D renders and animations as a side gig.


I always had a strong interest in areospace since I was young after attending my first airshow. I later found out about rocketry and had my goals set to one day work on them, which is why I am currently pursuing areospace engineering.


As for my artistic side, it really kick started around 2020 during the Covid-19 pandemic, as that was when I recieved my first tablet and stylus. I made a lot of digital art since then, and only recently did I upgrade my tablet from the crappy one the school gave me. Around the same time, I also picked up 3D rendering and animation after seeing lots animations of SpaceX's Starship on Youtube. You can find my 3D animations on my Youtube, most of which are rocketry related.


On a more personal note, i'm not much of a gamer and only really play 2 or 3 games: Umamusume, Kerbal Space Program and occasionally Wuthering Waves. I also like travelling overseas, more specifically to countries that snow a lot. I also do photography sometimes especially when i'm overseas.
